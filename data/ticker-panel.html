About

Bitcoin Ticker Plus Wallet Watcher, Currency Converter and QR Code Generator

This add-ons pricing data is Powered by CoinDesk, blockchain.info, Coinbase.com, BitPay.com and LocalBitcoins.com. You can adjust the update times in Preferences. You can add a bitcoin address to check the balance and recent transactions automatically. Also comes with a currency converter. Currency is formatted using toFixed(decimals) and toLocaleString(), results may vary. In some instances padding and rounding may occur. Converter uses 8 decimal places for BTC conversion. QR Codes are generated client-side (in browser) using jquery-qrcode.

This add-on only makes requests to these URLs.
  • https://blockchain.info/ticker
  • https://coinbase.com/api/v1/prices/spot_rate?currency= + selected currency
  • https://bitpay.com/api/rates
  • https://localbitcoins.com/bitcoinaverage/ticker-all-currencies/
  • https://blockchain.info/address/ + entered bitcoin address + ?format=json
  • https://api.coindesk.com/v1/bpi/currentprice/ + selected currency.json
  • https://ajax.googleapis.com/ajax/services/feed/load?q=http://feeds.feedburner.com/CoinDesk
  • http://www.reddit.com/r/Bitcoin/.json
